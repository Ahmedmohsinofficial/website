Who I Am

I'm an Egyptian designer who believes that design is more than visuals.
it’s a way to solve problems, tell stories, and create meaningful experiences.

My journey with design started early. As a child, I loved drawing, coloring, and children’s books filled with imagination and color. Over time, simple design programs became part of my world and marked the beginning of my passion for design.

In 2007, I joined Applied Arts to study Advertising & Graphics, where I graduated with a Bachelor’s degree with honors. This academic journey shaped my passion and turned it from a personal interest into a professional skill.
