Key Visual

Key visual design is a service that provides a clear message to the target audience, as images are often the most effective means of communication. The process typically involves creating multiple sketches to explore various ideas, presenting them to the client for selection, and then executing the chosen concept using manipulation techniques. Key visuals are commonly used in billboards, advertising campaigns, and as the main theme for events.

Medical Designs

Working on medical designs is not an easy task for any designer. It requires multiple skills and knowledge about different diseases, available treatment techniques, and diverse ways of thinking.
