About Me

Meet me

Who I Am?

My name is Ahmed Mohsin.
I’m an Egyptian designer, born in 1991, based in Cairo, Egypt.
I’m Muslim, married, and I believe that art is the most beautiful way to solve problems.

Let’s rewind

How it started?

Since I was a kid, I loved drawing, coloring, and children’s books full of colors and imagination.
Later, I started using simple design programs at a young age. They quickly became part of that world, and that’s where my love for design truly began.

Follow Me

How it grew?

In 2007, I joined Applied Arts to study Advertising & Graphics and graduated in 2012 with a Bachelor's degree with honors.
This academic journey gave structure to my passion and turned it into a skill with an academic foundation, shaping a professional who can deliver creative designs.
